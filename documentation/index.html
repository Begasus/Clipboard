The Clipboard Project Cut, copy, and paste anything, anywhere!

Clipboard by Jackson Huff is a power tool that lets you cut, copy, and paste anything, anywhere, all in the terminal.

Now you can do anything that involves moving information around a lot quicker and easier.

Why Choose Clipboard?

Quick. Zero configuration needed to use.
Easy. Friendly to newbies and power users alike.
Pretty. Breaks the rules to look beautiful.
Compatible. Works on anything that supports C++20.
Unified. Functions exactly the same everywhere.
Universal. Supports several languages.
Integrated. Connects with many native GUI clipboards.
Tiny. Mere tens of kilobytes in size.
